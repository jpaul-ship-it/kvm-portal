Email Notifications — Microsoft 365

Employees receive email when PTO is approved or denied. Requires Microsoft 365 / Outlook SMTP credentials.

ⓘ If MFA is enabled on your Microsoft account, create an App Password at account.microsoft.com/security and use that instead of your regular password.

Google Calendar — Time Off Sync

When PTO is approved, automatically add it to your company Google Calendar so managers can see who is off.

ⓘ Setup: 1. Create a Google Calendar for KVM Time Off → 2. Create a Service Account in Google Cloud → 3. Share the calendar with the service account email → 4. Paste the JSON key above.
Daily Attendance Emails

Sent automatically Monday–Friday. 7:00 AM — Field Technicians  |  9:00 AM — Office Staff.
Office departments: Management, Executives, Office Staff, Sales. All others are treated as field technicians.

ⓘ Uses current clock-in data as of right now. Good for testing before the scheduled time.
